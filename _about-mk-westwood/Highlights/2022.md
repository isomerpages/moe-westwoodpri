### **2022**
#### **K1 Learning Journey: Singapore Zoo**
With the easing of the Safe Management Measures, the K1 children had the opportunity to participate in a Learning Journey to the Singapore Zoo. The children were able to get up close with butterflies and various other animals at the Fragile Forest!&nbsp;  
  
To view the photos, please click&nbsp;[here](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1vUsPeVEOGkXK4ednTgDgM8pfCRUuerR5)

#### **K2 Learning Journey: Learning Forest @ Singapore Botanic Gardens**

With the easing of the Safe Management Measures, the K2 children had the opportunity to participate in a Learning Journey to the Learning Forest located at Singapore Botanic Gardens. Children were given various roles as they explored the different animals, insects and plants around them!  
  
To view the photos, please click&nbsp;[here](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/199C2epZCAqxG6j8jAKON6V2BObwcvRvd)
