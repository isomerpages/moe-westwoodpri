### **Waiting List**
Dear Parents/Guardians,

Thank you for your interest in enrolling your child/ward in Westwood Primary School.

Please complete the online Wait List Application Form at [https://go.gov.sg/wwps-wait-list-application](https://go.gov.sg/wwps-wait-list-application).  

Your application is valid for a year from the date of submission. If you do not hear from us by then, your application is considered unsuccessful.  You may wish to resubmit your application to be put on the wait list again for the following year.
