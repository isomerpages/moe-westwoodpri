### **P6 Graduation Day**
The primary 6 pupils of class of 2021 held their graduation day on 18 November 2021. Class representatives from each P6 class came together to plan out the overall programme with SMM in place. Each class recorded performances and speeches for their peers, teachers and school. The graduation day ended on a high note for the class of 2021! Happy Graduation, WWPS Class of 2021!
