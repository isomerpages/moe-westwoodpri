### **BCA Green Mark Award GOLDplus**

Leading the way in sustainability, Westwood Primary earns BCA Green Mark Award GOLDPLUS!

The Green Mark certification scheme was launched in January 2005, and it is a renewable award granted every five years. It is heartening that our efforts on adopting green technologies and practices have been well recognized.
