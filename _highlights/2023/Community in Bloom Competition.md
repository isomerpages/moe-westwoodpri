### **Community in Bloom Competition**

Heartiest congratulations to Gardens@Westwood Primary for being awarded the **'Top 50 CIB Garden'** in the **Community in Bloom** Competition!

Top 50 CIB Garden recognises community gardens that have beautiful and diverse gardens, actively engaging their communities, and are committed to sustainable and environmentally-friendly practices. 

Parents can access the [link](https://www.nparks.gov.sg/-/media/nparks-real-content/gardening/community-in-bloom/cib-awards/cib-awards-2023/cib-awards-2023-dossier.ashx) to view the details of the winners.

You can access the following [link](https://sites.google.com/moe.edu.sg/biodiversitywestwood/home?pli=1) to view the records of fauna sightings at Westwood Primary School.
