Our pupils were immersed in the vibrant spirit of Deepavali on 10 November 2023. There were cheers of joy during the celebration, as pupils enchanted with soulful songs, and the mesmerizing Hindu dance moves by our teachers brought the spirits high. A celebration where music and dance unite, leaving everyone in awe.
