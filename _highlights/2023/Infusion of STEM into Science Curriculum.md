### **STEM Sharing with Master Teachers**

On 14 February 2023, Westwood Primary did a sharing of how STEM is infused into our Science Curriculum and our learning points. Master Teacher in Science, Ms Tan Poh Hiang together with two Lead Teachers Ms Serene Han from Monfort Junior and Ms Hazel Tan from Fuhua Primary enjoyed their interactions with the student representatives.
