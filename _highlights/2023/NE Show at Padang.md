### **NE Show at Padang**

On 8 july, our P5 pupils set off to Padang in high spirits to watch the NE Show. Our pupils cheered and waved during the performances, celebrating Singapore's 58th birthday not only as One Westwood but One Singapore! May we “Onward As One” in many more years to come.
