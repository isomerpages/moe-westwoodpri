### **NSMark Gold Award**<img src="/images/NSmark%20Gold%20Logo.png" style="width:30%">


Westwood Primary is honoured to receive the NSMark Gold Award in recognition of our exemplary support
to Total Defence and National Service.

<img src="/images/NSMark%20Certificate.jpg" style="width:85%">

NS Mark is a national accreditation scheme that
recognises businesses and organisations for their support
of National Service (NS) and Total Defence (TD). By committing to the Declaration of Support, businesses and
organisations acknowledge the importance of National Service which underpins the continued stability and economic progress of Singapore.

We are indeed honoured to be accredited as responsible
corporate citizens and choice employers.
