### **Racial Harmony Day**

Pupils had a week of fun-filled activities to commemorate Racial Harmony Day on 21 July. Racial Harmony Day is commemorated to promote and celebrate racial harmony, unity, and understanding among people of different races and cultural backgrounds.

You may view the photos [here](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1-Idldqm8RRuhmdwS0ZHIZYgHiNZ9rw2k?usp=sharing) (link is accessible until 31 Aug).
