### **Parent Support Group**

If you have a keen interest in joining the Westwood Primary Parent Support Group (PSG) and wish to contribute to the school's activities, or take part in organizing workshops and events for parents and students, we invite you to sign up using this [online form](https://go.gov.sg/westwoodpri-psg-registration). We eagerly anticipate your membership in our PSG!

#### **WWPS PSG Mission Statement**
The WWPS Parent Support Group (PSG) strives to create a welcoming and inclusive environment for the WWPS family.

We embrace the school’s vision statement and work collectively, and in partnership with the school, to support the school’s endeavours.

We envision&nbsp; to develop a caring and encouraging environment for all&nbsp; students of Westwood Primary,&nbsp; collaborate with the stakeholders to support the learning and&nbsp; development outcomes of&nbsp; our flourishing pupils,&nbsp; so that they will have a heart of gratitude ,&nbsp; a resilient mind-set, a&nbsp; future-ready confidence, and&nbsp; a passion for the&nbsp; community.

##### **The WWPS PSG aims to:**
*   Establish a close partnership with the school in supporting its programmes and enhance student learning and&nbsp;&nbsp;school experiences.
*   Provide a platform for parents to build genuine friendship and provide opportunities for meaningful collaboration
*   Provide a platform for parents to share parenting skills and experiences.
*   Initiate and implement programmes that enhance parenting skills
*   Initiate and implement programmes that promote student well-being
*   Uphold the image and interest of the school
